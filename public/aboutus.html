Who are we?

We are a community of people committed to improving the world that we live in. We aim to spread awareness about the various climate change phenomena and their causes, and encourage the public to take steps like recycling, minimise wastage and dedicate their time for a good cause by participating in environmental campaigns.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to stop the degradation of the planet’s natural environment and build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature.
